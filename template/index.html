Gaussian Elimination Solver




Method Explanation

Gaussian elimination transforms a system of linear equations into upper triangular form, then solves using back substitution.

Example 1

Given system:

$$ \begin{cases} 6x - 2y - 4z = 16 \\ -2x + 10y - 8z = -40 \\ -4x - 8y + 18z = 0 \end{cases} $$

Step 1: Convert to augmented matrix

$$ \begin{bmatrix} 6 & -2 & -4 & | 16 \\ -2 & 10 & -8 & | -40 \\ -4 & -8 & 18 & | 0 \end{bmatrix} $$

Continue row operations...

Step 3: Back substitution

Final solution: x = 2, y = -2, z = 1
