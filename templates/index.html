What is Gaussian Elimination?

Gaussian elimination is a method used to solve systems of linear equations by transforming them into an upper triangular matrix and applying back substitution.

Example

$$ \begin{cases} x + y + z = 6 \\ 2x + 3y + z = 11 \\ 3x + y + 2z = 11 \end{cases} $$
